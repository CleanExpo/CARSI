# Assessment Framework

> How we check that a new starter has actually reached the standard — not just sat through the
> training. Assessment is **demonstrated competence**, signed off by a supervisor, with evidence
> recorded. This framework sits over the 12 modules (`01-module-structure.md`) and feeds the
> 30/60/90-day pathway (`04-pathways-30-60-90.md`).

## Principles

1. **Competence, not attendance.** A tick means "I watched them do it correctly," not "they were
   there."
2. **Two checks per module.** A **knowledge check** (understanding) and a **practical demonstration**
   (doing). Both must pass.
3. **Safety and reputation are non-negotiable.** Any unsafe act or confidentiality breach in a
   demonstration is an automatic fail for that check, regardless of the score.
4. **Evidence is recorded.** Knowledge-check result, demonstration sign-off, and any photos/forms go in
   the new starter's record.
5. **Re-assessment is normal.** A fail means coach and re-check — not a black mark. Repeated fails on the
   same competency are a development conversation.

> **Automatic-fail acts** (any module): performing a task without the PPE its SDS requires; bypassing a
> safety device (e.g. RCD); leaving a wet/just-sealed floor unsigned or un-barricaded in an occupied
> area; photographing a child, person, document or screen; using a chemical/method that would damage the
> surface. These fail the demonstration regardless of score and are re-coached before re-assessment.

## The two checks explained

### Knowledge check
- Delivered as the per-module "knowledge check" (in the LMS course, an inline Q&A lesson with an answer
  key; on paper, the sample questions in each module).
- Multiple choice plus short scenario questions.
- Pass mark per the table above. Below the mark → review the module and re-sit.

### Practical demonstration
- The new starter performs the task on a real or mock job while a supervisor observes against the
  relevant checklist/standard.
- Graded **Competent / Not yet competent** against the practical standard in the table.
- "Not yet competent" → supervisor coaches the specific gap and re-observes. No partial credit on
  safety-critical items.

      exercise, signed confidentiality acknowledgement)

Use `checklists/supervisor-sign-off.md` as the single recording form across all 12 modules.

## Final practical assessment (Module 12)

The gate to working independently. Assessed on a **full standard job**, end to end:

- Vehicle/equipment readiness → site arrival & setup → surface identification → correct method &
  chemical → safe machine operation → hazard control → professional conduct → confidentiality/security →
  close-out with evidence and handover.

**Result:**
- **Pass:** ≥85% overall **and** 100% on every safety-critical and reputation-critical item (no
  automatic-fail acts). Supervisor signs the independent-operator clearance.
- **Not yet:** coach the specific gaps, keep the new starter on supervised work, and re-assess. There is
  no time pressure to pass — competence comes before independence.
