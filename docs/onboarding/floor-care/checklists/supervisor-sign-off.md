# Floor-Care Supervisor Competency Sign-Off

Per-module sign-off form. A supervisor confirms the worker has demonstrated each competency before the worker is cleared to work independently.

> Australian English. Dates DD/MM/YYYY. Currency AUD.
> This form records demonstrated practical competency. It makes no medical, health, insurer or compliance-authority claims. Dilution rates, cure/re-entry times and slip-resistance details come from the SDS/TDS and relevant standards, not from this form.

## How to use this form

- For each module, mark **Demonstrated (Y/N)**, note **evidence sighted**, and confirm the **pass standard** was met.
- Supervisor initials + date go in each block.
- Pass standards:
  - **Modules 3, 6, 7, 8 (safety-critical) and Module 10:** 90%
  - **All other knowledge modules:** 80-85%
  - **Module 12 (final practical):** 85% overall, with 100% on all safety-critical and reputation-critical items.
- "Demonstrated" means seen in practice or on a real/simulated job — not just talked about.
