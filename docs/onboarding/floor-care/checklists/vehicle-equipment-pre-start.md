# Vehicle & Equipment Daily Pre-Start Checklist

**Purpose:** A daily, before-you-leave check so the vehicle, TruckMount, machines, tools, PPE and consumables are ready for today's jobs — completed at the depot before departure.

> **Core principle: If it is not checked, it can stop the job.**
> A job rarely fails from poor cleaning. It fails because the vehicle isn't ready, the TruckMount can't get to site, a machine won't start, the wrong tool was left behind, PPE or chemical is missing, a battery is flat, or a hose is damaged. This check is how you stop that happening.

> **Backup principle: carry spares.** Always travel with spare pads, brushes, fuses, and enough of the key chemicals to finish the day. A small spare can save a whole job.

> **VIC note:** If operating in Victoria, work under the Victorian *Occupational Health and Safety Act 2004* and its regulations, not the model WHS laws. Confirm local depot procedures. **[COMPANY TO CONFIRM]**

## 2. TruckMount Readiness *(if fitted)*  *[COMPANY TO CONFIRM fit-out]*

- [ ] TruckMount starts and reaches operating temperature / pressure
- [ ] Fuel for the TruckMount unit adequate (separate from vehicle if applicable)
- [ ] Solution tank filled; chemical injection set correctly
- [ ] Waste / recovery tank empty and not cracked
- [ ] Pressure and heat within normal range on gauges
- [ ] Hose reel runs free; enough hose length for likely access **[COMPANY TO CONFIRM reach]**
- [ ] No leaks at pump, fittings or tanks
- [ ] Vacuum / recovery working at the wand
- [ ] Access route to site usable for the hose run (consider distance, stairs, doors)

## 3. Machines Present & Working

- [ ] Rotary machine — present, runs, no unusual noise
- [ ] Auto scrubber — present, runs, tanks intact
- [ ] Burnisher — present, runs
- [ ] Wet vacuum — present, runs, suction OK
- [ ] Portable extractor — present, runs, sprays and recovers
- [ ] Pressure system — present, builds pressure, no leaks
- [ ] All machines clean and free of obvious damage / cracked housings

## 4. Batteries

- [ ] All cordless machine batteries charged
- [ ] Spare battery packs charged and packed
- [ ] Battery / scrubber chargers packed (if charging on site)
- [ ] No swollen, hot or damaged battery packs — set any aside, do not use

## 5. Hoses, Leads & Electrical

- [ ] Vacuum / solution hoses checked end to end — no splits, kinks or damaged cuffs
- [ ] Extension leads checked — no cuts, exposed conductors or damaged plugs
- [ ] Portable RCD / safety switch present and packed
- [ ] **Press the RCD test button** — it trips and resets correctly
- [ ] Leads and equipment within their test-and-tag status (see maintenance checklist)
- [ ] Couplings and quick-connects seat firmly, no leaks

## 6. Floor Tools, Pads & Brushes  *(matched to today's jobs)*

- [ ] Wands, hand tools, edging tools for the surfaces booked today
- [ ] Pads — correct grades/colours for the floors and tasks booked
- [ ] Brushes — correct type (soft / medium / aggressive / grout) for the jobs
- [ ] Squeegees and blades in good condition
- [ ] Mops and buckets — clean, present, with wringer
- [ ] Spare pads and brushes packed (backup principle)

## 7. Chemical Stock  *(enough for the day)*  *[COMPANY TO CONFIRM chemical list]*

- [ ] Enough of each product for today's jobs, plus a margin
- [ ] All containers sealed, not leaking
- [ ] Every container labelled on the body (not the lid), including decants
- [ ] Incompatible chemicals (acids vs alkalis / oxidisers) segregated on the vehicle
- [ ] Current SDS available for each product carried (see chemical & PPE checklist)
- [ ] Key chemicals carried as spares so the day can be finished

## 8. PPE

- [ ] PPE for today's tasks packed, selected per each product's SDS
- [ ] Gloves (correct type), eye protection, respiratory protection as required
- [ ] Safe footwear, hi-vis as required by site
- [ ] PPE clean and serviceable — no torn gloves, scratched eyewear or expired respirator filters

## 9. Signage & Barriers

- [ ] Wet-floor / caution signs packed (enough for the areas booked)
- [ ] Barriers / cones / tape packed
- [ ] Signs clean and legible

## 10. Safety & Emergency Gear

- [ ] Spill kit present and complete
- [ ] First-aid kit present, stocked and in date
- [ ] Fire extinguisher present, charged and **in date** (check gauge / tag)
- [ ] Eyewash / water available if required by the chemicals carried

## 11. Tool Inventory vs Today's Job List

- [ ] Run today's job list against the load — every job's specific tools, pads, brushes and chemicals are on board
- [ ] Any specialty / hire item for a specific job is loaded
- [ ] Keys, access cards, site instructions and paperwork packed **[COMPANY TO CONFIRM access system]**
- [ ] Nothing on the job list is missing from the vehicle
