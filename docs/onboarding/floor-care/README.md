# Professional Floor Care Onboarding & Operational Readiness Program

> **This onboarding program teaches staff that professional floor care is not just cleaning. It is a
> safety, hygiene, presentation, asset-protection, and operational-readiness service. Every
> technician must understand the surfaces, chemicals, equipment, risks, site expectations, and
> professional standards required to deliver consistent results in sensitive facilities.**

This pack is the master onboarding program for technicians, supervisors and facilities staff who
deliver floor surface cleaning, polishing, sealing, maintenance and hygiene services across schools,
childcare and education facilities, public buildings, commercial sites and other high-traffic
environments.

It is written in Australian English and in plain trade language. It is built to be used as a real
onboarding document, training manual, induction pack, supervisor checklist set, and the source
content for a future LMS course.

## How to use it

- **New technician:** work through `00`, then the modules in `01`, and keep the `checklists/` to hand
  on the job. Your supervisor signs off your competency using `checklists/supervisor-sign-off.md`.
- **Supervisor:** run inductions from `checklists/staff-induction.md`, train against `01`, assess
  against `03`, and move new starters along the `04` 30/60/90 pathway.
- **Management / HR / safety officer:** start at `00` for the why and the standard, then use `02`
  and `05` to plan rollout and ongoing operational control.

## Guardrails this pack is written to

- **Australian English** throughout; dates DD/MM/YYYY, currency AUD.
- **No overclaiming.** We do not claim accreditation, insurer recognition, or compliance-authority
  status, and we make no medical claims. Floor care **supports** hygiene, **reduces** soil load,
  **helps manage** contaminants, and **contributes to** safer indoor environments — it does not cure,
  sterilise, or guarantee health outcomes.
- **Standards and SDS are the authority.** This training supports good judgement; it does not replace
  a product's Safety Data Sheet / Technical Data Sheet, Australian Standards, a site's own rules, or a
  qualified professional. Cure times, re-entry times, dilution ratios and slip-rating targets come
  from the SDS/TDS and the relevant standard, not from this document.
