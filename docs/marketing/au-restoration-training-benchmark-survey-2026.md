# Australian Restoration & Cleaning Training Benchmark Survey 2026 — programme pack

**Status:** instrument DRAFT for founder approval · **Owner:** CARSI · **Flagship authority play**
(wiki signal: ATMC's US training-benchmark survey grew participation ~65% YoY, CollisionWeek
2026-06-10 — nobody owns the Australian restoration/cleaning equivalent).

**Why:** the citable dataset play. Search and AI engines now reward being the *source* — a yearly
AU benchmark makes CARSI the reference every article, LLM answer, RTO conversation and insurer
panel discussion cites. Results feed course positioning, sales narratives, and PR for years.

## Method (keep it defensible)
- Anonymous online survey, ~7 minutes, 22 questions. Company-level answers, one response per firm.
- Population: AU restoration, carpet/commercial cleaning, IAQ businesses (owners/ops managers).
- Distribution: CARSI list · CCW customer list · INCLEAN readership approach · RIA Australasia ·
  supplier partners · LinkedIn. Target n ≥ 150 firms for credible cuts by state + segment.
- Incentive: respondents get the full report 4 weeks before public release (no prize draws —
  keeps the data clean and the ACL story simple).
- Publication: headline report (public, citable, with charts + methodology page), full cut for
  respondents, media pack. All claims reproducible from the dataset (cite-or-cut applies to us).

## Instrument v0.1 (22 questions)

**Firmographics (Q1–Q5)**
1. Primary work: restoration / carpet & upholstery cleaning / commercial cleaning / IAQ-mould / mixed
2. State/territory (multi) · 3. Technician headcount band (1–4 / 5–14 / 15–49 / 50+)
4. Years operating band · 5. Insurance-panel work share (none / <25% / 25–75% / >75%)

**Training investment (Q6–Q10)**
6. Formal training hours per technician in the last 12 months (band)
7. Annual training spend per technician (AUD band)
8. Who delivers most training: in-house / supplier / online provider / in-person school / none
9. Paid training time: on the clock, partly, or technician's own time
10. Change vs last year: spend up / flat / down — and the main reason (free text, short)

**Certification mix (Q11–Q14)**
11. Share of technicians holding any IICRC certification (band)
12. Which IICRC certifications are most common in your team (multi: WRT/ASD/AMRT/FSRT/OCT/CCT/RRT/other)
13. How do certified staff maintain certification: CEC courses / retesting / lapse-and-recertify / unsure
14. Does certification affect pay or charge-out rates in your business? (yes-pay / yes-rates / both / no)

**Operations reality (Q15–Q19)**
15. Hardest role to hire in the last year (free text, short)
16. Technician turnover in the last 12 months (band)
17. Biggest barrier to more training: cost / time off tools / relevance / travel / none (rank top 2)
18. WHS training beyond induction in the last 12 months? (yes/no + topic multi: PPE, mould, asbestos-awareness, manual handling/CT prevention, working at heights)
19. Has documentation quality (photos, moisture logs, scope notes) affected a claim outcome for you in the last year? (helped / hurt / both / not sure)

**Forward look (Q20–Q22)**
20. Are you using AI tools in operations today? (none / admin / quoting-scoping / documentation / marketing)
21. Skills you most need available as short online AU courses (free text)
22. Would you participate in an annual AU benchmark like this? (yes/no + email opt-in for the early report — stored separately from answers)

## Compliance notes
- Survey copy uses the compliant "IICRC Continuing Education Credit (CEC) courses" phrasing only;
  no CEC-hours claims; no implication CARSI certifies (per the licence rule).
- Privacy: responses anonymous; opt-in email stored separately; AU Privacy Act APP5 notice on the
  landing page. No respondent-identifiable publication.
- Report claims must be computable from the dataset (n, method, and date on every chart).
