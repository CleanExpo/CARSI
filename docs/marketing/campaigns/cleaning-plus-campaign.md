# Cleaning Plus Campaign — CARSI LMS

## GP-198 | Priority: P1 High

## Objective

Convert commercial cleaning business owners into restoration-trained contractors by positioning IICRC credentials as the bridge from cleaning to higher-margin restoration work.

**Targets:**

- 15–25 qualified leads/month by month 3
- 20% lead-to-enrolment conversion rate
- CPL (cost per lead): $60–90 AUD
- Average order value: $795 AUD (annual subscription) or $20–45 per individual course

---

## Campaign Concept: "Cleaning Plus"

**Core Insight:** Commercial cleaning contractors already have the client relationships, equipment familiarity, and on-site access. They're one credential away from offering restoration services — and charging 3–5x more per job.

**Tagline Options:**

1. "You're already on-site. Start restoring."
2. "From cleaning to restoration — the $200/hr upgrade."
3. "Your cleaning crew is one credential away from restoration revenue."

## Value Proposition

### The Revenue Gap

| Service                  | Typical Rate | Annual Revenue (1 crew) |
| ------------------------ | ------------ | ----------------------- |
| Commercial cleaning      | $35–55/hr    | $70K–$110K              |
| Water damage restoration | $150–250/hr  | $200K–$400K             |
| Mould remediation        | $120–200/hr  | $180K–$350K             |
| Carpet restoration       | $80–150/hr   | $130K–$250K             |

**Key message:** "Same clients. Same sites. 3–5x the hourly rate."

### Why IICRC Credentials Matter

1. **Insurance panel access** — Insurers require IICRC-certified contractors for restoration claims
2. **Government tender eligibility** — State/federal contracts mandate industry certifications
3. **Client confidence** — Property managers and facilities teams prefer credentialed providers
4. **Legal protection** — Documented competency reduces WHS liability exposure

### The CARSI Advantage for Cleaning Companies

| Challenge                              | CARSI Solution                                |
| -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| "Can't take staff off-site for a week" | 24/7 online, self-paced, 30-minute modules    |
| "Training costs are too high"          | From $20/course or $795/year unlimited        |
| "Don't know where to start"            | Guided IICRC pathways (WRT → CRT → OCT)       |
| "Need proof for tenders"               | Instant verifiable credentials + CEC tracking |
| "Staff turnover means retraining"      | Annual subscription covers unlimited staff    |
