# Construction Industry Campaign — CARSI LMS

## GP-142 | Priority: P1 High

## Objective

Penetrate the Australian construction sector with IICRC restoration training, targeting site managers, project managers, and WHS officers across commercial and residential construction.
Target: 600 enrolled construction professionals in 12 months.

## Target Audience

- Site managers and project managers at tier-1 construction companies
- Building certifiers and compliance officers
- WHS (Work Health & Safety) officers
- Key companies: CIMIC, Lendlease, John Holland, Multiplex, Mirvac
- 376,000+ residential builders and trades across Australia

## IICRC Disciplines Relevant to Construction

| Discipline                           | Application                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| WRT — Water Damage Restoration       | Water ingress during construction, wet concrete slabs, waterproofing failures     |
| ASD — Applied Structural Drying      | Structural drying for wall cavities, concrete, timber framing                     |
| AMRT — Applied Microbial Remediation | Mould remediation in new builds (30%+ of new QLD homes have mould within 2 years) |

## Key Value Propositions

1. **NCC Compliance** — Training supports National Construction Code obligations for moisture and mould management
2. **WHS Act Obligations** — Employers must ensure workers can identify and respond to mould and water hazards
3. **Contractor Pre-Qualification** — Verifiable digital credentials for pre-qualification panels at tier-1 builders
4. **Defect Liability Reduction** — Trained site staff reduce mould and water damage defect claims during the liability period
