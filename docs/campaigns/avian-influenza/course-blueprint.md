# CARSI course blueprint — Australian H5 bird flu readiness

## Course title

Australian H5 Bird Flu Awareness for Restoration, IAQ and Facility Professionals

## Positioning

This is a calm awareness course. It follows Australian Government wording and is not veterinary, medical or government-response training.

## Required public message

If sick or dead birds or animals are found, avoid contact, record what you see and report it to the Emergency Animal Disease Hotline on 1800 675 888.

## Modules

1. Australian H5 status and official reporting pathway.
2. Professional boundaries for restoration, cleaning, IAQ and facility teams.
3. WHS, PPE, hygiene, exposure records and clean/dirty zones.
4. Cleaning-before-disinfection principles and documentation.
5. Product and dry-fogging claim limits: label, SDS, regulatory review and authority directions first.
6. RestoreAssist.app field records: photos, PPE logs, cleaning records, exceptions and completion reports.
7. Client and media communication without panic language.
