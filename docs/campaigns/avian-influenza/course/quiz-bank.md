# Quiz Bank — Australian H5 Bird Flu Awareness

## Passing score

Recommended pass mark: 80%.

## Question 1

A member of the public reports a dead bird near a public walkway. What is the first approved message?

A. Bag the bird and bring it to the office.
B. Do not touch it, avoid the area, record the location and call 1800 675 888.
C. Spray the area immediately and post online.
D. Tell the client the site has bird flu.

Correct answer: B

Rationale: The public reporting message is to avoid contact, record what is seen and use the Emergency Animal Disease Hotline.
