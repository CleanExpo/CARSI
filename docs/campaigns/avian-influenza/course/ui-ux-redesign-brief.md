# UI/UX Redesign Brief — Australian H5 Readiness Course

## Purpose

Use the OpenAI-generated course layout concepts as the visual direction for the CARSI LMS experience. The aim is to replace generic course-page styling with a polished professional learning experience that feels like a public-safety briefing and a credible industry training hub.

## Design principles

1. Calm first. The interface should reduce anxiety and clarify the next step.
2. Professional restoration context. The design should feel practical and field-ready.
3. Official-source hierarchy. Government and reporting guidance must be visually separated from commercial calls to action.
4. Strong module progression. Learners should always know where they are, what is next and what has been completed.
5. Documentation mindset. The RestoreAssist workflow should appear as a practical field-record pathway.
6. No fake authority. The page must not imply CARSI, Synthex, RestoreAssist or NRPG are government agencies.
7. No generic AI decoration. Avoid random icons, empty metrics, glass panels and noisy dashboards.

## Recommended course landing layout

### Above the fold

- CARSI course brand strip.
- Title: Australian H5 Readiness.
- Short description: awareness course for restoration, IAQ, cleaning and facility professionals.
- Primary reporting panel: Report first — 1800 675 888.
- Secondary CTA: Start course.
- Supporting CTA: View source register.

### Mid-page

- Module tiles with progress status.
- Source register preview.
- Synthex agency of record note.
- Ivi Sims contact block.

### Footer

- CARSI, Synthex, RestoreAssist, DisasterRecovery and NRPG ecosystem links.
- Correction policy and source review date.

### Centre column

### Right column

## Recommended assessment layout

- Clear question progress.
- One question per screen.
- Plain language answer options.
- Review rationale after answer.
- Completion progress panel.
- Source/evidence review panel.
- SME review status panel.

## Accessibility requirements

- WCAG AA colour contrast.
- No text embedded as the only source of information in images.
- Every image requires descriptive alt text.
- Mobile first: lesson content must stack before secondary panels.
- Keyboard focus visible.
- No hover-only interactions.

## Content guardrails

- Hotline remains visible in public reporting and lesson screens.
- Ivi Sims contact: 1300 654 684 and supplied LinkedIn profile only.
- No direct email unless explicitly approved.
- No synthetic Ivi portrait.
- Product content must remain label-led, SDS-led and authority-aware.
