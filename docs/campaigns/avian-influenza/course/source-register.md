# Course Source Register — Australian H5 Bird Flu Awareness

## Source policy

This course must be checked against official and technical sources before publication. If official guidance changes, course content must be corrected before further promotion.

## Course source rules

- Use government and public-health sources first.
- Use product websites for manufacturer terminology only unless label, SDS and regulator evidence are available.
- Do not cite social posts as primary evidence.
- Do not use AI-generated content as evidence.
- Do not present commercial claims as official guidance.

## Review cadence

- Daily during the first week of campaign launch.
- Weekly during the first 30 days.
- Immediately if DAFF, Australian CDC, state/territory authorities or Animal Health Australia release updated advice.
